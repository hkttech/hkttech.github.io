  • Basics

  • How to play as guest?

    In the login page, click on the yellow Play button.

  • How to play with Facebook Account?

    In the login page, click on Save My Progress button if you play as guest. You will be direct to facebook page to login, and then you can save your progress with Facebook Account

    Or in the login page, click on Play with Facebook button. You will be direct to facebook page to login, and then you can play with Facebook Account

  • Account

  • How to save game data?

    Your game data will be automatically updated to server if your phone has network connection.

    If you play as guest, your data will be lost if you switch phone.

    If you play with Facebook, your data will be automatically saved and synced even you switch phone.

  • How to delete my account?

    Step 1: Click on the Setting menu at the bottom

    Step 2: In the Setting page, click on the Delete Account button. It will open a confirmation window

    Step 3: Click on the Delete button in the confirmation window
